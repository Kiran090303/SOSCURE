What is SOSCURE?

Despite our independence, we still face a problem. As many of us get assaulted while travelling alone, and when they cry out for help, there is no one nearby to assist them or give them the help that they need, SOSECURE is application that offer security to everyone irrespective of gender.


About us

'Where there is a will there's a way' Due to some events in my life I was stunned and was very deeply regretted by this happen to me which made me question our mankind. I can feel an uncertain feeling while traveling alone . I got this sensation of that somebody is watching me, and this outcomes in uneasiness , anxiety about being distant from everyone else and self-uncertainty to most extreme. So, in this entire cycle I got to know my team members and with their assistance and our commitment we fostered the answer name as “SOSCURE” for not totally tackle this issue , yet to give desire to the people who actually has fear in their heart from going out alone.


Our Vision as SOSCURE

Vision of our idea is to make travel more secure and safer for any individual worried while travelling alone. Soon we will keep on further developing this application to make it more reliable to the public and also continue to improve safety and security measures.
